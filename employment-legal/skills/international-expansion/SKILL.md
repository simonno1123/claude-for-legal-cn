# International Expansion - Phase 2 Stub

## Status

This reference skill is suspended for the China Mainland employment-law Phase 1 release.

Do not use the original international expansion framework, offshore employer/entity decision tree, foreign parent company assumptions, permanent establishment prompts, or foreign employment-law checklists as substantive guidance for China Mainland employment-law work.

## Safe behavior

If loaded by another skill, respond in Chinese:

> 涉外用工/境外雇佣扩张属于 Phase 2 暂缓能力。本模块不输出 EOR、境外实体设立、境外劳动法、税务或移民结论。请由目标法域当地律师、税务顾问和移民顾问确认；本工具仅可整理用户提供的待核验问题清单。

- 明确标注所有实体法律结论均待当地专业人士确认。
